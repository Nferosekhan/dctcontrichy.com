Curtain Raiser of the Grand 14th Annual Professional Conference of the Diabetes Club of Tiruchirappalli - DCTCON 2024

OUR ENVISION

To ensure that the Diabetologists, Physicians and General Practitioners of Trichy stay abreast on the latest Evidence-based Outcomes on Diabetes Research from around the World.

To enhance and disseminate knowledge on understanding Diabetes and its associated Complications through regular CME Workshops, Seminars, Public Outreach Programs and Detection Campaigns.

OBJECTIVES

The Diabetes Club of Tiruchirappalli is a distinguished Academic Forum dedicated to achieving excellence in the field of Diabetes by actively Organizingvarious Health Education Programmes for Medical Practitioners and the General Public. Our Inter-Professional Team of Experts collaborates to share their expertise in Diabetes Care, aiming to implement best Practices and introduce new tools to enhance the delivery of streamlined services.

Our Mission is to foster Academic interest, Fellowship andSolidaritywithin the Medical Community, particularly in the realm of Diabetology.

We aim to provide comprehensive Medical Aid and support to benefit underprivileged Populations, regardless of their background and are dedicated to promoting Health Education and well-being among the vulnerable Community.

Our initiatives encompass creating a Library exclusively for Members, acquiringcutting-edge Medical Equipment, hosting Conferences and sponsoring Members forTraining and Research Events in Diabetology, offering financial assistance to impoverished Patients and awardingScholarships and Stipends to deserving Medical Students.

HONOUR

Since its inception in 2009, the Diabetes Club of Tiruchirappalli, recognized under the Societies Act, has ceaselessly pursued its Goal. This remarkable Journey has been made possible by the unwavering support of Our respected Patrons, Executive Members and devoted DCT Members.

The establishment of DCT was Orchestrated by a distinguished team of experienced Senior Physicians, led by the Legendary Clinician Late Dr.B.Bosco, alongside esteemed colleagues Dr.G.Surendra Babu, Dr.S.Sethuraman, Dr.N.S.Prasad, Dr.ML.Balamurugan, Dr.V.Ravindranath and Dr.R.Vijayakumar are all resolutely enthusiastic about realizing the Noble Quest of DCT.

Contemplating the Educational voyage of DCT, We are privileged to have had Dr.B.Bosco as the Inaugural Chairman, Dr.N.S.Prasad as the Secretary andrevered Patrons Dr.S.Ponniah, Dr.M.S.Ashraf and Dr.M.Shunmugvelu lending their Wisdom and guiding Our path. Their leadership and support have been instrumental in shaping the success of Our Organization.

The 1st Scientific Symposium was gracefully inaugurated by Our iconic Dr.M.S.Ashraf, where renowned Cardiologist Dr.M.Chenniappan captivated the audience with his enlightening presentation. More than 50 Physicians were gathered at the initial meeting and EC Members were meticulously chosen from among the Delegates in attendance. This ensures that Our Institution is a Hub of exceptional Caliber and Expertise.

It's delightful and a source of great pride that Our Club has seen a significant rise in its Life Membership reaching 160 Members enrolled thus far. Our Vision is Ambitious, yet Achievable and We are committed to making a difference.

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

  • Every 2nd Tuesday of the Month is dedicated to the CME Programs discussing different aspects of Diabetes and Complications by Healthcare Professionals.
  • The Annual Professional Conference DCTCON is being conducted every Year.

DCTCON is a banquet of Scientific knowledge, Networking andInnovationcentered around the latest advancements in Diabetes Research and Treatment. The Objective we hold dear is one of Exquisite Elegance and Unwavering Persuasion.
